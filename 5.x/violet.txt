Notes :
• Firmware Included
• Rom is encrypted by default
• Gapps and Vanilla both available
• Use latest Twrp A13 by @Joker_V2_0 or Orrangefox
• For vanilla build Nikgapps

Flash Instructions :
• Wipe (dalvik, Caches,system,vendor,data) 
• Flash ROM zip
• Flash GApps (If u flash Vanilla Build) 
• Flash FcryptV4(Decryption)
• Format data(Format if u are encrypted)
• Boot into System.
